Some Remarks

It is important to notice the two kinds of induction used in these proofs :
  • Induction on nat in the proof of : 
     mult2_even : forall n:nat, even (mult2 n).
  • Induction on (even n) in the proof of : 
    sum_even :forall n p:nat, even n -> even p -> even (n + p).

square_even : forall n:nat, even n -> even (n * n).
